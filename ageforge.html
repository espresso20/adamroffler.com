What is AgeForge?

AgeForge is a full terminal-based idle empire builder written in Go that runs entirely in your terminal. No browser needed. No install wizard. No cloud account. Just download a single binary, open your terminal, and start building civilizations that span from the dawn of stone tools to transcendent post-human existence.

With 22 ages of progression, deep resource management, technology trees, military expeditions, trade diplomacy, and a prestige system that rewards long-term play, AgeForge delivers real strategy depth in a package that fits inside any terminal window.

Game Systems

Buildings & Wonders

284 buildings across 22 ages including 22 unique Wonders that grant permanent civilization bonuses and unlock the next age.

Technology Tree

52 technologies with prerequisite chains that multiply production, unlock new mechanics, and reveal hidden paths forward.

Military Expeditions

15 missions with escalating risk and reward. Send soldiers to raid ruins, defend trade routes, and conquer new lands.

Trade & Diplomacy

15 trade routes across 6 NPC factions. Negotiate, ally, or rival. Diplomatic status directly affects exchange rates.

Prestige System

Sacrifice your civilization for permanent meta-progression upgrades. 9 tiers of 5 levels each carry across all future runs.

Catastrophes & Legacy

Every 3 ages, face epoch-defining catastrophes. Endure, succumb, or defer. Each choice shapes your civilization's legacy.
